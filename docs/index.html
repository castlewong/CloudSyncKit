Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 2, 2026

Short version: Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Any data synced via iCloud stays entirely within your personal iCloud account, under Apple's control.

Overview

This privacy policy applies to all iOS and macOS apps developed and published by castlewong. It describes how our apps handle your data.

Data We Do Not Collect

Our apps do not collect, process, or transmit any data to any server operated by us or any third party. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, Apple ID, or device ID)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or diagnostics
  • Crash reports
  • Any app data or user content

iCloud Sync

Some of our apps offer optional iCloud sync to keep your data in sync across your devices. When enabled, all data flows exclusively between your device and your private iCloud account:

  • Data is stored in your private iCloud container, accessible only to you
  • No data passes through any servers owned or operated by us
  • iCloud sync is optional and can be disabled at any time in the app settings
  • iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13, or any personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via GitHub.
